About ToolBox

Financial Toolbox is a free app that puts you in control of your finances. It's like having a personal financial coach in your pocket, helping you with everyday tasks and long-term goals.

Powerful Calculators at Your Fingertips

Investment Calculator: Figure out potential returns on your investments.

Mortgage Calculator: Easily estimate your monthly mortgage payments.

Loan Calculator: Compare loan options and make informed borrowing decisions.

Present & Future Value Calculator: Understand the time value of money.

Retirement Planner: Start planning for a secure and comfortable retirement today.

 

And More to Come! The Financial Toolbox team is constantly adding new features to help you manage your money even better.
